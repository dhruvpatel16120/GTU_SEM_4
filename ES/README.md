## 📖 Official Syllabus Units (BE04000101)

> **TIP**
> **Units 3 and 4** account for **60%** of the weightage; focus on Sustainability and Renewable Energy for max scoring.

| Unit       | Title & Major Topics                                                                               | Hours | Weightage |
| ---------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----- | --------- |
| **Unit 1** | **Introduction to Environment:** Principles, Scope, Impacts of Technology, Conservation.           | 2h    | 5%        |
| **Unit 2** | **Environmental Pollution:** Water, Air, Noise, and Land Pollution, Solid/Bio-medical/E-waste.     | 10h   | 20%       |
| **Unit 3** | **Sustainability:** Circular Economy, Climate Change, Green Building Principles, Concept of 4 R’s. | 9h    | 30%       |
| **Unit 4** | **Renewable Energy:** Conventional vs Renewable, Solar, Wind, Biomass, Green Hydrogen technology.  | 9h    | 30%       |

### 💡 Pro-Tips for High Marks:

> **Focus on Mitigation Strategies!** Diagrams of treatment processes and waste management lifecycles are crucial for high marks.

- 📌 **Pollution Classes:** Differentiate between sources of common pollutants like PM, SO2, and NOx carefully.
- 📌 **Sustainability:** Learn the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) as they are frequently tested.
- 📌 **4R’s:** Be ready to give real-world industrial application examples for the 4R’s logic.
- 📌 **Renewable:** Focus on Green Hydrogen – it's a new and emerging high-frequency topic.
